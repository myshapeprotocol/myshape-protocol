### Data Promise

**What we capture:**
- Motion signal (joint-position wireframe, 30fps × 30s)
- Presence receipt (entropy proof: PoP + MP + EP)
- Device type & lighting condition
- Session timestamp

**What we never capture:**
- Raw camera images or video
- Facial data or biometric templates
- Personal identity documents
- IP address or location
- Any data that could reconstruct your appearance or identity

**All motion processing runs on-device.** Raw signal never leaves your machine. Only the cryptographic proof is transmitted.
